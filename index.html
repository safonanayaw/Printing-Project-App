Project Description

Our ambitious project mirrors the functionality of leading printing platforms such as vistaprint.com and solopress.com. It empowers users to seamlessly upload their artwork, integrate it into a variety of products like business cards and flyers, and even provides access to a repository of system-provided artwork.

List of Features

  • Intuitive Online Payment System: Secure credit card transactions or seamless Momo payment integration.
  • Streamlined Onboarding Process: User-friendly registration and login processes for seamless customer onboarding.
  • Advanced Artwork Upload: Support for various file formats, including AI, PSD, and high-resolution images.
  • Dynamic Product Integration: Smooth integration of uploaded artwork into diverse products, allowing real-time previews.
  • Editable Templates for Personalization: User-friendly customization options, enabling customers to add personal details to templates.
